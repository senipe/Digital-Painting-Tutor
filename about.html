About Me

A Bit About Me

I've been passionate about digital painting for years. There's something magical about bringing ideas to life on a screen - the way colors blend, how light plays across a surface, and the endless possibilities that digital tools offer. I love the process of creating, from that first rough sketch to the final polished piece.

After getting my BA (Hons) in Concept and Comic Art, I've worked as a concept artist in the gaming industry and as a freelancer. But what I really enjoy is sharing this passion with others and helping them discover their own creative voice through digital art.
